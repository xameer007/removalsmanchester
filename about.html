Our Story

About Manchester Removals

Since 2010, we've been Manchester's trusted partner for professional removals, helping thousands of families and businesses relocate with confidence.

Manchester's premier removal company

Manchester Removals was founded with a simple mission: to make moving stress-free for everyone in Greater Manchester. What started as a small man and van service has grown into one of the region's most trusted removal companies.

We understand that moving home or office is one of life's most significant events. That's why we treat every removal with the same care and attention, whether it's a student flat move in Fallowfield or a corporate relocation in Manchester city centre.

Our team of professionally trained removal specialists brings decades of combined experience to every job. We know Manchester intimately – from navigating the Northern Quarter's cobbled streets to handling parking permits in Didsbury's residential areas.
